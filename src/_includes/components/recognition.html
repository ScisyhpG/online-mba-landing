King’s Own Institute (KOI) is registered with the Tertiary Education Quality and Standards Agency (TEQSA), Australia's independent national regulator for higher education.

This registration confirms that the KOI Online MBA meets rigorous Australian Qualifications Framework (AQF) standards, ensuring academic integrity, industry relevance, and robust quality assurance.

For students, this means confidence that your MBA is nationally recognised, respected by employers, and delivers a qualification with genuine value in today’s competitive job market.
